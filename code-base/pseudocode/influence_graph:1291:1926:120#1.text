1. Set up a bowl
2. Add flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt to the bowl
3. Mix the dry ingredients together
4. Add milk, eggs, and melted butter to the bowl
5. Mix the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients until smooth
6. Heat a griddle or frying pan to medium high
7. Pour the batter onto the griddle
8. Cook until the edges start to dry and the surface is bubbly
9. Flip the pancake and cook until golden brown on both sides
10. Serve hot with desired toppings.
