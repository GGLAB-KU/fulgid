1. Input food into mouth
2. Chew food and mix with saliva
3. Swallow food, which travels down the esophagus
4. Food enters the stomach
5. Stomach acid breaks down food further
6. Food travels into the small intestine
7. Nutrients from food are absorbed into the bloodstream
8. Food travels into the large intestine
9. Remaining material is considered waste
10. Waste is eventually removed from the body.
