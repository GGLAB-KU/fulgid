1. Set up a heating apparatus to heat water.
2. Pour saltwater into a container.
3. Place the container on the heating apparatus.
4. Heat the water until it evaporates.
5. Collect the evaporated water using a condenser.
6. Collect the salt left behind in the container.
7. Repeat steps 2-6 until desired amount of salt is removed.
8. Dispose of the salt properly.
