1. Load coal into firebox
2. Boiler produces steam under high pressure
3. Steam flows down into cylinder
4. Piston is pushed by steam
5. Piston is connected to locomotive's wheels through crank and connecting rod
6. Crank and connecting rod turn locomotive's wheels
7. Train is powered along
8. Outlet valve opens
9. Piston pushes steam back through cylinder
10. Steam exits through locomotive's chimney.
