1. Mix pancake mix with water
2. Heat griddle
3. While griddle is heating:
   a. Prepare butter and syrup
4. Pour a circle of batter onto griddle
5. Wait for bubbles to form on surface of pancake
6. Check if most of the bubbles have popped
7. If most of the bubbles have popped, flip pancake
8. Cook pancake until desired color is achieved
9. Remove pancake from griddle
10. Apply butter and syrup to pancake
11. Enjoy pancake
