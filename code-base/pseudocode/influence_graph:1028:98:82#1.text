1. If temperature < desired temperature:
2.    Send signal to heating system
3.    Oil travels from tank to burner
4.    Oil is mixed with air to create a fine mist
5.    Inject oil/air mixture into burner
6.    Ignite burner in combustion chamber
7.    Furnace heats air
8.    Blower sends heated air through ducts
9.    Heated air comes out of vents and into room.
