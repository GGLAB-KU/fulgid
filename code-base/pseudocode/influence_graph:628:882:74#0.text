1. If blood sugar levels rise:
   a. Release insulin into the bloodstream
2. If blood sugar levels are too low:
   a. Release glucagon into the bloodstream
3. Over 99% of exocrine pancreas cells produce digestive juices
4. Enzymes for breaking down fats, proteins, and carbohydrates are present in the digestive juices
5. Digestive juices flow from the pancreas through an excretory duct into the small intestine.
