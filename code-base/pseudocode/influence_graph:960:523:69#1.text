1. Obtain coal from the ground
2. Pulverize coal into fine powder
3. Mix coal powder with hot air
4. Blow coal and hot air mixture into a boiler
5. Ignite coal and hot air mixture to create a fire
6. Pump highly purified water through pipes in the boiler
7. Water turns into steam due to the heat from the fire
8. Steam presses against turbines
9. Turbines spin due to the pressure from the steam
10. Electricity is produced as a result of the spinning turbines.
