1. Input sugar into the body
2. Sugar enters the bloodstream
3. The pancreas detects high sugar levels in the bloodstream
4. The pancreas releases insulin to lower sugar levels
5. Insulin travels through the bloodstream to reach the cells
6. Insulin signals the cells to absorb sugar from the bloodstream
7. Blood sugar levels decrease
8. The pancreas detects normal blood sugar levels
9. Insulin production decreases
10. Blood sugar levels stabilize at normal levels.
