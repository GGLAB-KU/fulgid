1. Take food into mouth
2. Chew food
3. Saliva mixes with food
4. Saliva breaks down food
5. Food travels down esophagus to stomach
6. If system is weakened by stress, then skip to step 8
7. Stomach secretes acid, grinds and mixes food
8. Food moves to small intestine
9. Small intestine uses enzymes to break down food
10. Food passes into colon
11. Food is expelled from body
