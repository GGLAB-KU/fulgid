1. Take air through the mouth
2. Air enters the lungs
3. Oxygen fills the sacs in the lungs
4. Carbon dioxide is removed from the lungs and exhaled
5. Oxygen is transported throughout the body.
6. If bladder releases urine, it does not directly affect the amount of oxygen reaching the body. However, if the release of urine causes dehydration, it can lead to a decrease in blood volume and affect the delivery of oxygen to the body's tissues.
