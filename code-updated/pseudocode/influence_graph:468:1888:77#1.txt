1. Input: Salt water in a container
2. Heat the salt water
3. While the salt water is being heated:
   a. The water becomes a gas
   b. The salt particles sink to the bottom of the container
4. Pipe the gaseous water into another container
5. While the gaseous water is being piped:
   a. If the gas condenses back into water, return to step 2
6. Allow the gaseous water to cool
7. The salt particles are left behind in the first container
8. Output: Fresh water in the second container
