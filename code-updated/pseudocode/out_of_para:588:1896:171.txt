1. Butterfly lays an egg
2. Larvae matures inside of the egg
3. Caterpillar hatches from the egg
4. While caterpillar is not fully grown:
   a. If engine is not getting enough air:
      i. Less caterpillars eat
      ii. Less caterpillars grow
   b. Else:
      i. Caterpillar eats
      ii. Caterpillar grows
5. Caterpillar spins a coccoon around itself
6. Caterpillar forms into a pupa inside the coccoon
7. Butterfly forms inside of the coccoon
8. Mature butterfly emerges from the coccoon
9. Butterfly mates
10. Female butterfly lays eggs.
