1. Bird picks a tree
2. Bird collects twigs and other refuse
3. Bird makes a flat base with twigs
4. Bird makes sides with twigs and other things
5. Bird adds softer material to the inside
6. If the weather is bad:
   a. Bird collects more twigs and refuse to make a larger nest
   b. Bird reinforces the sides and base with stronger materials
   c. Bird adds more insulation to the inside
7. Nest is done.
