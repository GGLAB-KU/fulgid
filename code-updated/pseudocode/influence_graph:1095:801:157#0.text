1. Initialize star with hydrogen fuel
2. While star has hydrogen fuel:
   a. Star burns hydrogen
3. Star becomes a red giant
4. While red giant has fuel:
   a. Red giant burns fuel
5. Red giant burns itself out
6. Core of star collapses
7. Gasses disperse
8. Only core remains
9. If the core mass is less than 1.4 solar masses:
   a. Core becomes a white dwarf
10. Else if the core mass is between 1.4 and 3 solar masses:
   a. Core becomes a neutron star
11. Else:
   a. Core becomes a black hole
