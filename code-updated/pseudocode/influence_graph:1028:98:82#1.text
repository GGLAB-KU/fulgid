1. If temperature < desired temperature:
2.    Calculate required amount of oil/air mixture based on the difference between current temperature and desired temperature
3.    Send signal to heating system
4.    Oil travels from tank to burner
5.    Oil is mixed with air to create a fine mist in the required amount
6.    Inject oil/air mixture into burner
7.    Ignite burner in combustion chamber
8.    Furnace heats air
9.    Blower sends heated air through ducts
10.    Heated air comes out of vents and into room.
