1. Mix pancake mix with water
2. Check if person is alive
3. If person is alive, heat griddle
4. While griddle is heating:
   a. Prepare butter and syrup
5. Pour a circle of batter onto griddle
6. Wait for bubbles to form on surface of pancake
7. Check if most of the bubbles have popped
8. If most of the bubbles have popped, flip pancake
9. Cook pancake until desired color is achieved
10. Remove pancake from griddle
11. Apply butter and syrup to pancake
12. If person is dead, turn off griddle and seek appropriate assistance
13. Enjoy pancake.
