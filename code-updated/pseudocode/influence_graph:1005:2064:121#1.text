1. Initialize tray
2. Add water to tray
3. If water level is less than required:
   a. Display "Add more water to tray"
4. Insert tray into freezer
5. While temperature of water is above freezing point:
   a. Water molecules slow down
6. Water freezes into ice
7. If ice cubes are less than expected:
   a. Display "Less ice cubes were made due to less water added to tray"
8. Ice cubes are ready
