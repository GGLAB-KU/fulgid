1. If weather is good:
   a. Animals eat a lot
   b. Animals gain weight
2. If weather is calm:
   a. Animals prepare to hibernate
      i. Animals find a place to hibernate
      ii. Animals enter hibernation:
         1. Body processes slow down
         2. Heart rate and breathing rate slow
         3. Weight loss slows down
         4. Animals can survive without eating or drinking without harm.
   b. Calm waters can help animals to hibernate properly.
