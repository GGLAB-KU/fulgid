1. Input food into mouth
2. Chew food and mix with saliva
3. If choking occurs, stop eating and seek medical attention
4. If no choking occurs, swallow food, which travels down the esophagus
5. Food enters the stomach
6. Stomach acid breaks down food further
7. Food travels into the small intestine
8. Nutrients from food are absorbed into the bloodstream
9. Food travels into the large intestine
10. Remaining material is considered waste
11. Waste is eventually removed from the body.
