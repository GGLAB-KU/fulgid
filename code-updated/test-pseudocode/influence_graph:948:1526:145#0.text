1. Set initial amount of water on earth
2. While there is water on earth:
   a. Water evaporates from the earth quickly
   b. The evaporated water condenses into water drops
   c. The water drops fall back to earth
   d. The water accumulates on the earth
   e. If less water collects into bodies:
      i. Reduce the amount of water collected into bodies
   f. The water soaks into the earth
3. End loop
