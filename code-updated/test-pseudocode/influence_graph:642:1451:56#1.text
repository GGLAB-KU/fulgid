1. Procedure: Frog Life Cycle
2. Input: Water safety status
3. Output: Fully grown frog
4. Begin
5.   If water is safe then
6.     Lay thousands of eggs
7.     Egg clusters float around in water
8.     Hatch tadpoles with gills from the eggs
9.     Tadpoles swim in water until they grow legs
10.    Absorb their tails
11.    Tadpole becomes a froglet
12.    Froglet becomes a frog
13.    Output fully grown frog
14.  Else
15.    Print "Water is too dangerous for frogs to survive"
16.  End If
17. End Procedure
