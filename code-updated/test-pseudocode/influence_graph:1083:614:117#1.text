1. Dig multiple holes
2. Place pumpkin seeds in each hole
3. Cover the seeds with dirt
4. Water the seeds
5. Wait for the seeds to sprout
6. Once the seeds have sprouted, vines will grow
7. Wait for the vines to flower
8. Pumpkins will appear where the flowers were
9. If there are more pumpkin seeds, the pumpkins may be smaller due to competition for resources
10. If there are fewer pumpkin seeds, the pumpkins may be larger due to less competition for resources.
