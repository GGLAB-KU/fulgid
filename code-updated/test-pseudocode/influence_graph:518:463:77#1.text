1. AdultFish lays eggs
2. Some eggs may not hatch due to various reasons
3. Incubated eggs hatch into young fish
4. The number of young fish may be less due to unhatched eggs
5. Young fish remain in hatching area until they are big enough to venture into open water
6. Fish continue to grow
7. Fish reach adulthood
8. Adult fish reproduce
9. Cycle continues from step 1
