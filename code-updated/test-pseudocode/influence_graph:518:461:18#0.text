1. AdultFish lays eggs
2. Eggs incubate for a few weeks or months
3. Eggs hatch into young fish
4. Young fish remain in hatching location until they are big enough to venture into open water
5. Fish continue to grow
6. Fish reach adulthood
7. Adult fish reproduce, laying eggs and continuing the cycle.
8. If there are more fish eggs, the number of young fish will increase
9. The increased number of young fish may lead to competition for resources such as food and space
10. If the competition for resources becomes too intense, some fish may not survive
11. The surviving fish will continue to grow and reproduce, perpetuating the cycle.
