1. Adult frog spawns eggs in water
2. Eggs hatch into tadpoles
3. Tadpoles develop external gills and a longer tail
4. Tadpole begins storing food in tail
5. Tadpole develops hind legs and lives off food stored in tail
6. Front legs appear and tadpole's tail shortens
7. Tadpole now looks like a young frog
8. If poor weather occurs, then there will be fewer tadpoles
9. Young frog continues to grow to adulthood.
