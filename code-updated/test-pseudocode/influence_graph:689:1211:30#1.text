1. Input: seeds
2. Sow seeds into the ground
3. Input: water and fertilizer
4. Apply water and fertilizer to the earth where the seeds are planted
5. Seedlings start to grow
6. Input: water
7. Water the plants regularly
8. Seedlings grow into plants
9. Remove weeds growing near the plants
10. If less sunny days occur:
11. Check if there is enough space for the plants to grow
12. If there is not enough space, consider transplanting some plants to a different location
13. Continue to water and care for the plants until they reach maturity.
