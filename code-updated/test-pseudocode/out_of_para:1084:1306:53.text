1. Get pumpkin seeds
2. Plant pumpkin seeds in soil
3. Water plant if soil starts to dry out
4. Do not water directly on leaves
5. Monitor for any human encroachments on the land
6. If human encroachments occur, take measures to protect the pumpkin plants
7. Wait for male flowers to appear
8. Monitor for any changes in the number of male flowers due to human encroachments
9. Wait for female flowers to open (they are open for one day)
10. Monitor for any changes in the number of female flowers due to human encroachments
11. Wait for pumpkins to mature (85-125 days after planting)
12. Monitor for any changes in the growth or quality of pumpkins due to human encroachments.
