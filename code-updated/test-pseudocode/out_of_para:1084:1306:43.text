1. Get pumpkin seeds
2. Plant pumpkin seeds in soil
3. Water plant regularly, especially during drought conditions
4. Do not water directly on leaves
5. Wait for male flowers to appear
6. Wait for female flowers to open (they are open for one day)
7. Wait for pumpkins to mature (85-125 days after planting)
8. Monitor plants closely during drought conditions for signs of stress or disease
9. Consider implementing additional measures such as mulching or shade cover to protect plants during extreme drought.
