1. Frog lays eggs in water
2. Tadpoles develop inside eggs
3. Eggs hatch
4. While tadpoles are growing:
   a. Tadpoles eat and grow
   b. Tadpoles develop legs
5. Tadpoles form into frogs
6. If bad weather conditions occur:
   a. Evaluate the impact on the environment
   b. Determine if the environment can support the frogs
   c. If the environment cannot support the frogs:
      i. Fewer tadpoles will survive
      ii. Fewer frogs will form
      iii. Fewer frogs will leave the water
   d. If the environment can support the frogs:
      i. Tadpoles will continue to grow and form into frogs
      ii. Frogs will leave the water as usual.
