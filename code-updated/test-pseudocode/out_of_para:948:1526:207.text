1. Set initial amount of water on earth
2. Set initial amount of bees in the area
3. While there is water on earth:
   a. Water evaporates from the earth
   b. The evaporated water condenses into water drops
   c. The water drops fall back to earth
   d. The water accumulates on the earth
   e. If the amount of bees in the area is greater than the initial amount:
         i. Less water accumulates on the earth
      Else:
         i. The water collects into bodies of water
         ii. The water soaks into the earth
4. End loop
