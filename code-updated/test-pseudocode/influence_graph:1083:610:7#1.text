1. Dig a hole
2. Place a pumpkin seed in the hole
3. Cover the seed with dirt
4. If there is no water source nearby, use a watering can or transport water to the site
5. Water the seed
6. Wait for the seed to sprout
7. Once the seed has sprouted, a vine will grow
8. If there is no water source nearby, use a watering can or transport water to the site to water the vine
9. Wait for the vine to flower
10. Pumpkins will appear where the flowers were
11. If there is no water source nearby, use a watering can or transport water to the site to water the pumpkins
12. Note that smaller pumpkins may result from a lack of water.
