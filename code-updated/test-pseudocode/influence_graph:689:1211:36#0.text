1. Input: seeds
2. Sow seeds into the ground
3. Give the earth where the seeds are planted water and fertilizer
4. Wait for seedlings to grow
5. Input: water
6. Give water to the plants
7. Wait for the seedlings to grow into plants
8. Remove weeds growing near the plants
9. If more seeds of weeds are available:
   a. Increase the frequency of weeding
   b. Allocate more space for plants by removing additional weeds
10. Wait for the plants to grow to maturity.
