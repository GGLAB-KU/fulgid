1. Fish lays eggs
2. Eggs hatch
3. For each egg:
   a. Tiny fish called fry emerge
   b. Fry eat and grow
4. If there are more predators who prey on fry:
   a. Some fry may be eaten and not survive
   b. The number of surviving fry may decrease
5. When fry become adult fish:
   a. If there are fewer surviving fry:
      i. Fewer adult fish may be produced
      ii. Fewer fish may mate
   b. If there are more surviving fry:
      i. More adult fish may be produced
      ii. More fish may mate
6. Repeat from step 1.
