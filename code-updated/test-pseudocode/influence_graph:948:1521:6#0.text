1. Set initial amount of water on earth
2. While there is water on earth:
   a. Water evaporates from the earth
   b. The evaporated water condenses into water drops
   c. The water drops fall back to earth
   d. The water accumulates on the earth
   e. The water collects into bodies of water
   f. The water soaks into the earth
   g. If flood or heavy rain conditions occur:
      i. Increase the amount of water that accumulates on the earth
      ii. Increase the amount of water that collects into bodies of water
      iii. Decrease the amount of water that soaks into the earth
   h. Update the amount of water on earth
3. End while loop.
