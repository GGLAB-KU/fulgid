1. Check weather conditions
2. If weather is bad, reduce the likelihood of bees landing on flowers
3. If bees do land on flowers, little hairs on their legs may not attach to pollen sacs from stamens of flowers due to the bad weather
4. Bees may not fly away with pollen due to the bad weather
5. If bees do fly away with pollen, they may not be able to land on other flowers due to the bad weather
6. If bees do land on other flowers, the pollen sacs may not be rubbed off onto the stigma of the new flower due to the bad weather.
