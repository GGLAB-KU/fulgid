1. Dig a hole
2. Place a pumpkin seed in the hole
3. Cover the seed with dirt
4. Water the seed
5. Wait for the seed to sprout roots
6. If the seed does not sprout roots, it is a dead seed and the procedure ends
7. Once the seed has sprouted roots, wait for the seed to sprout above ground
8. Once the seed has sprouted above ground, a vine will grow
9. Wait for the vine to flower
10. Pumpkins will appear where the flowers were
