1. Set initial amount of water on earth
2. While there is water on earth:
   a. Water evaporates from the earth
   b. If located near the ocean:
      i. Check if wind is blowing towards the ocean
      ii. If wind is blowing towards the ocean, less water falls back to earth
      iii. If wind is not blowing towards the ocean, continue with normal procedure
   c. The evaporated water condenses into water drops
   d. The water drops fall back to earth
   e. The water accumulates on the earth
   f. The water collects into bodies of water
   g. The water soaks into the earth
3. End loop
