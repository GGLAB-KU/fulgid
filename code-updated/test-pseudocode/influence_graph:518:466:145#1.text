1. AdultFish lays Eggs
2. Eggs incubate for a few weeks or months
3. If ocean temperature increases, fewer eggs may hatch
4. Eggs that do hatch develop into YoungFish
5. YoungFish remain in hatching area until they are big enough to venture into open water
6. Fish continue to grow
7. Fish reach adulthood
8. AdultFish reproduce, laying eggs and continuing the cycle.
