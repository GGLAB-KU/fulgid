1. Fish lays eggs
2. Eggs hatch
3. For each egg:
   a. Tiny fish called fry emerge
   b. Fry eat and grow
4. For each fry:
   a. If fewer tadpoles survive:
      i. Less food is available for the fry
      ii. Fry growth is stunted
   b. Fry become adult fish
   c. Fish mate and more eggs are produced.
