1. AdultFish lays Eggs
2. Eggs incubate for a few weeks or months
3. Eggs hatch into YoungFish
4. YoungFish remain in hatching area until they are big enough to venture into open water
5. Fish continue to grow
6. Fish reach adulthood
7. If there is a disease amongst the fish population:
   a. The disease affects some of the fish
   b. The affected fish may die or become weaker
   c. The weaker fish may be preyed upon by predators
   d. The disease may spread to other fish in the population
8. AdultFish reproduce, laying eggs and continuing the cycle.
