1. Dig a hole
2. Place a pumpkin seed in the hole
3. Cover the seed with dirt
4. Water the seed
5. Monitor the amount of rain received
6. If there is a lack of rain, supplement with additional watering
7. If there is a lack of rain, the pumpkins may be smaller in size
8. Wait for the seed to sprout
9. Once the seed has sprouted, a vine will grow
10. Wait for the vine to flower
11. Pumpkins will appear where the flowers were
12. If there is a lack of rain, continue to supplement with additional watering to promote healthy growth and larger pumpkins.
