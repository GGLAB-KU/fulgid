1. Check if it is pumpkin season
2. If it is pumpkin season, proceed with the following steps:
   a. Dig a hole
   b. Place a pumpkin seed in the hole
   c. Cover the seed with dirt
   d. Water the seed
   e. Wait for the seed to sprout
   f. Once the seed has sprouted, a vine will grow
   g. Wait for the vine to flower
   h. Pumpkins will appear where the flowers were
3. If it is not pumpkin season or the ground is too hard to dig a hole, skip the above steps and end the procedure.
