1. Check if the area is good for planting pumpkins
2. If the area is good, dig a hole
3. Place a pumpkin seed in the hole
4. Cover the seed with dirt
5. Water the seed
6. Wait for the seed to sprout
7. Once the seed has sprouted, a vine will grow
8. Wait for the vine to flower
9. Pumpkins will appear where the flowers were
10. If the area is good for planting pumpkins, the pumpkins may grow larger or produce more pumpkins.
