1. FemaleFish lays Eggs
2. Eggs are laid in a nest
3. Egg nest is uncovered by water currents
4. If egg nest is uncovered, it may affect the survival of the eggs and result in fewer hatching
5. If fewer eggs hatch, there will be fewer larvae
6. Larvae feed and grow
7. Larvae develop into JuvenileFish
8. JuvenileFish grow into AdultFish
9. AdultFish mate
10. FemaleFish lays Eggs
