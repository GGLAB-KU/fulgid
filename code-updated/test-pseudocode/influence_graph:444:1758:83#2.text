1. Check if weather is suitable for bees
2. If weather is suitable, more bees will be present
3. Bees land on flowers to gather nectar
4. Little hairs on the bees legs attach to pollen sacs from the stamens of the flowers
5. When the bees fly away, the pollen goes with the bees
6. More bees mean more flowers are visited, increasing the chances of pollen transfer
7. The bees land on other flowers
8. The pollen sacs are rubbed off onto the stigma of the new flower
9. More seeds will travel with bees due to increased pollen transfer.
