1. Input: Different kinds of rocks
2. Process: 
   a. Rocks melt into magma
   b. If more rock is melted:
      i. Increase the amount of magma produced
   c. Magma cools in the crust
   d. If less magma is available to cool:
      i. Decrease the amount of rock melted
   e. Magma rises to the surface and becomes lava
   f. Lava cools
   g. Cooled magma and lava become igneous rock
3. Output: Igneous rock
