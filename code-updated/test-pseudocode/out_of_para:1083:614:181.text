1. Dig a hole
2. Place a pumpkin seed in the hole
3. Cover the seed with dirt
4. Water the seed
5. Wait for the seed to sprout
6. Once the seed has sprouted, a vine will grow
7. If less magma gets to the surface, the pumpkins may be smaller or fewer in number
8. Wait for the vine to flower
9. Pumpkins will appear where the flowers were
10. If less magma gets to the surface, the pumpkins may be smaller or fewer in number
