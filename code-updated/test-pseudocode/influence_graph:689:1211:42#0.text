1. Input: seeds
2. Sow seeds into the ground
3. Give the earth where the seeds are planted water and fertilizer
4. Wait for seedlings to grow
5. Input: water
6. Give water to the plants
7. Wait for the seedlings to grow into plants
8. If weeds grow near the plants:
   a. Remove the weeds
   b. Check if there is enough space for the plants to grow
   c. If there is not enough space, remove some plants to make room for the remaining plants
9. Wait for the plants to grow to maturity.
