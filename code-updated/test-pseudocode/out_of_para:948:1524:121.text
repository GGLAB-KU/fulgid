1. Set initial amount of water on earth
2. While there is water on earth:
   a. Water evaporates from the earth
   b. The evaporated water condenses into water drops
   c. The water drops fall back to earth
   d. If plates are being pushed together:
      i. Increase the frequency of water drops falling back to earth
   e. The water accumulates on the earth
   f. The water collects into bodies of water
   g. The water soaks into the earth
   h. Update the amount of water on earth
3. End while loop.
