1. If weather is good for bees:
   a. While bee is flying:
      i. Land on flower
      ii. Eat nectar
      iii. Pollen gets on fur
      iv. Fly to another area with flowers
      v. Deposit more pollen on flowers in new area
2. Else:
   a. Follow the regular procedure:
      i. While bee is flying:
         1. Land on flower
         2. Eat nectar
         3. Pollen gets on fur
      ii. Fly to another area with flowers
      iii. Deposit pollen on flowers in new area
      iv. Plant in new area is able to reproduce.
