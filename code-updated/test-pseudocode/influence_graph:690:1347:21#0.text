1. Choose crop
2. Till soil
3. Spread seeds
4. Till again to plant seeds
5. If rains fall regularly during growing season:
     6.1. Reduce watering frequency
     6.2. Monitor crops for signs of overwatering
     6.3. Adjust fertilizer application as needed
     6.4. Watch for signs of disease or pests
     6.5. Harvest crops as they mature
6. Else:
     7.1. Water crops as needed
     7.2. Monitor crops for signs of under-watering
     7.3. Adjust fertilizer application as needed
     7.4. Watch for signs of disease or pests
     7.5. Harvest crops as they mature
8. Wait for crops to grow
