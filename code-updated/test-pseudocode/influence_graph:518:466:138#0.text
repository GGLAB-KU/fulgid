1. AdultFish lays Eggs
2. Eggs incubate for a few weeks or months
3. If there are fewer predators, then more eggs are likely to hatch
4. Eggs hatch into YoungFish
5. YoungFish remain in hatching location until they are big enough to venture into open water
6. Fish continue to grow
7. Fish reach adulthood
8. AdultFish reproduce, laying Eggs and continuing the cycle.
