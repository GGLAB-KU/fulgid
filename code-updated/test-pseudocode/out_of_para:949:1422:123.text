1. Get water from a lake or ocean
2. Check the water level of the lake
3. If the water level is low, adjust the amount of water taken from the lake
4. Boil the water to evaporate it
5. Allow the water vapor to enter the atmosphere
6. Accumulate the water vapor in the atmosphere
7. Form clouds from the accumulated water vapor
8. Check if the clouds become too heavy
9. If the clouds become too heavy, initiate precipitation
10. End procedure.
