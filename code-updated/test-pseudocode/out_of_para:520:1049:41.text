1. FemaleFish lays Eggs
2. Eggs hatch into Larvae
3. If pressure is large enough to push to the surface:
    a. Larvae may be exposed to less food
    b. Larvae may have difficulty eating and growing
4. Larvae form into JuvenileFish
5. JuvenileFish grow into AdultFish
6. AdultFish mate
7. FemaleFish lays Eggs
