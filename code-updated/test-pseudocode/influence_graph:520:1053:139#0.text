1. FemaleFish lays Eggs
2. Eggs hatch into Larvae
3. Larvae eat and grow
4. Larvae form into JuvenileFish
5. JuvenileFish grow into AdultFish
6. AdultFish mate
7. FemaleFish lays Eggs
8. If water is polluted:
   a. Decrease the number of eggs laid by FemaleFish
   b. Decrease the survival rate of Larvae
   c. Decrease the growth rate of JuvenileFish
   d. Decrease the survival rate of AdultFish
   e. Decrease the mating rate of AdultFish
   f. Repeat steps 1-7 with the updated parameters.
