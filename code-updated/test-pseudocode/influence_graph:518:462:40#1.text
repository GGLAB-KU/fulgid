1. AdultFish lays eggs
2. Eggs incubate for a few weeks or months
3. Eggs hatch into young fish
4. Young fish remain in hatching area until they are big enough to venture into open water
5. Fish continue to grow
6. Fish reach adulthood
7. Adult fish reproduce
8. If more fish get disease:
   a. Isolate infected fish to prevent spread
   b. Treat infected fish to prevent further spread
   c. Monitor remaining fish for signs of disease
   d. Adjust feeding and environmental conditions to promote fish health
9. If less fish are affected by disease:
   a. Continue to monitor for signs of disease
   b. Adjust feeding and environmental conditions to promote fish health
10. Cycle continues from step 1.
