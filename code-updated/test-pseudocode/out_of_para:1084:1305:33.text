1. Get pumpkin seeds
2. Plant pumpkin seeds in soil
3. Water plant if soil starts to dry out
4. Avoid watering directly on leaves
5. Monitor the number of flowers in bloom
6. If there are many flowers in bloom, consider thinning them out to promote larger pumpkins
7. Wait for male flowers to appear
8. Watch for female flowers to open (they are only open for one day)
9. Wait for pumpkins to mature (this can take 85-125 days after planting)
