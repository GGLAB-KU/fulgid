1. Get pumpkin seeds
2. Plant pumpkin seeds in soil
3. Water plant if soil starts to dry out
4. Do not water directly on leaves
5. Wait for male flowers to appear
6. Wait for female flowers to open (they are open for one day)
7. If kids like to garden, ensure they do not overcrowd the plants
8. If plants are overcrowded, pumpkins may be smaller in size
9. Wait for pumpkins to mature (85-125 days after planting)
