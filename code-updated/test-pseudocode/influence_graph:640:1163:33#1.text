1. Fish lays eggs
2. Eggs hatch
3. For each egg:
   a. Tiny fish called fry emerge
   b. Fry eat and grow
4. For each fry:
   a. If there are less predators who feed on fry:
      i. Fry have a higher chance of survival
      ii. Fry grow faster
   b. Fry become adult fish
5. For each adult fish:
   a. Fish mate and more eggs are produced. 
6. Repeat from step 1.
