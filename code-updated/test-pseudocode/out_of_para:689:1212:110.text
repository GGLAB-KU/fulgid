1. Input: seeds
2. Sow seeds into the ground
3. Input: water and fertilizer
4. Apply water and fertilizer to the earth where the seeds are planted
5. Seedlings start to grow
6. Input: water
7. Water the plants regularly
8. Seedlings grow into plants
9. Remove weeds growing near the plants
10. Plants grow to maturity
11. If the size of the rocky area grows, increase the amount of water and fertilizer applied to the earth
12. Monitor the growth of the crops and adjust the amount of water and fertilizer as needed to ensure healthy growth
13. Harvest the mature crops and repeat the process for new seeds.
