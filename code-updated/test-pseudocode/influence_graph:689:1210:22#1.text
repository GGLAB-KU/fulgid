1. Input: seeds
2. Sow seeds into the ground
3. Give the earth where the seeds are planted water and fertilizer
4. Wait for seedlings to grow
5. Input: water
6. Give water to the plants
7. Wait for the seedlings to grow into plants
8. If plants grow smaller than expected:
   a. Check for any signs of disease or pests
   b. Adjust fertilizer and water levels as needed
   c. Monitor the plants closely for any further issues
9. Remove weeds growing near the plants
10. Wait for the plants to grow to maturity
11. Evaluate the impact of smaller plants on crop yield and adjust planting and care strategies as needed for future crops.
