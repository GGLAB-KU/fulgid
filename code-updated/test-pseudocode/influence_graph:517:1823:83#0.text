   a. There will be more eggs that hatch into tadpoles
2. Frog eggs hatch
3. Tadpole breaks free
4. While (6 weeks have not passed)
   a. Gills slowly disappear
5. After 8 weeks
   a. Hind legs begin to form
6. After 12 weeks
   a. Front legs form
7. Tail shrinks
8. Mouth grows larger
9. Tadpole can live on land as a frog
