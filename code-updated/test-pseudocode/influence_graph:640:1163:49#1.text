1. Fish lays eggs
2. Eggs hatch
3. For each egg:
   a. Tiny fish called fry emerge
   b. Fry eat and grow
4. For each fry:
   a. Fry become adult fish
   b. Fish mate and more eggs are produced.
5. If more fry become adult fish:
   a. The population of adult fish increases
   b. More fish are available for mating and producing eggs
   c. The cycle of egg laying, hatching, and growth repeats with a larger population of fish.
