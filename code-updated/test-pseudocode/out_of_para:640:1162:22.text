1. Fish lays eggs
2. Eggs hatch
3. For each egg:
   a. Tiny fish called fry emerge
   b. Fry eat and grow
4. For each fry:
   a. Fry become adult fish
   b. Fish mate and more eggs are produced.
5. If there is more water on the ground:
   a. Fish population may increase due to more breeding opportunities
   b. More food sources may become available for the fish
   c. Water quality may improve, leading to healthier fish
   d. However, if the water becomes too deep or turbulent, it may be difficult for the fish to survive.
