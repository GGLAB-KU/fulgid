1. Fish lays eggs
2. If soil is unproductive:
   a. Less food is available for the fish
   b. Fish may produce fewer eggs
3. Else:
   a. Eggs hatch
   b. For each egg:
      i. Tiny fish called fry emerge
      ii. Fry eat and grow
   c. For each fry:
      i. Fry become adult fish
      ii. Fish mate and more eggs are produced.
