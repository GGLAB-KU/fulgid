1. Dig a hole
2. Place a pumpkin seed in the hole
3. Cover the seed with dirt
4. Water the seed
5. Wait for the seed to sprout
6. Once the seed has sprouted, a vine will grow
7. If the plates are less active, the pumpkins may be smaller
8. Wait for the vine to flower
9. Pumpkins will appear where the flowers were, but they may be smaller if the plates are less active.
