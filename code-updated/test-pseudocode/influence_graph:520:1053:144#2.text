1. FemaleFish lays Eggs
2. Eggs hatch into Larvae
3. Larvae eat and grow
4. Larvae form into JuvenileFish
5. JuvenileFish grow into AdultFish
6. If PeopleEatLessFish:
       - AdultFish population decreases
       - Less AdultFish mate
7. Else:
       - AdultFish mate as usual
8. FemaleFish lays Eggs
