1. Frog lays eggs in water
2. Tadpoles develop inside eggs
3. Eggs hatch
4. While tadpoles are growing:
   a. If weather slows the growth of tadpole food:
      i. Tadpoles may not have enough food to eat
      ii. Tadpoles may grow more slowly
   b. Else:
      i. Tadpoles eat and grow normally
      ii. Tadpoles develop legs
5. Tadpoles form into frogs
6. Frogs leave the water
7. If tadpoles did not have enough food to eat:
   a. Some tadpoles may not survive to become frogs
   b. There may be fewer frogs that leave the water
